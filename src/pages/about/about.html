About This is an Ionic demo project to show the SilverStripe 4 GraphQL Headless powers using Ionic. It fetches the users from the demo-endpoint ReadMembersQueryCreator which is called on the Staff page with the readMembers query. In src/app/app.module.ts, the GraphQL endpoint is hardcoded. Update it to work with your endpoint. To adjust to another call, and/or have the data from a different call, adjust the query to your needs. From the demo files from SilverStripe/silverstripe-graphql, you can use paginatedReadMembers to paginate the members To change the data displayed, have a look at the available data in the call and update your requirements as needed in staff.ts and staff.html For more information on Ionic, visit the Ionic documentation.
